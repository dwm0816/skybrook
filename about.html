About the organization

The Skybrook foundation for sarcoidosis awareness is a small organization dedicated to raising awareness for sarcoidosis, and help victims of sarcoidosis and their families with the potential fallout of the disease.

The foundation was founded in spring of 2017 by cofounders Jeffery Dupuy, a long time sufferer of Sarcoidosis, David McKissick and Phillip Lambert.

The foundation was ultimately formed with the goal of global education and awareness of the disease thru long term awareness campaigns across the globe. As of now we are planning a campaign for schools across the country distributing materials teaching the basics of Sarcoidosis, how to spot it, and how it is treated.

If you would like to donate your time or equipment, contact us at donations@skybrookfoundation.org
